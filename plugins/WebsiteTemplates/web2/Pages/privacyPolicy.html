Privacy policy

Email

At Signet, Inc., we are strongly committed to protecting your privacy. We never disclose, share or sell client e-mail addresses. E-mail addresses are used exclusively for populating the billing/shipping screen while shopping online and at times for notifications, promotions or communications directly to specific clients or catalog/company store program constituents only.

Credit Card Information

Every precaution is taken to protect your credit card information and we want you to feel totally comfortable when you order from this site. The SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) is the most powerful encryption available on the Internet. Any information related to your credit card is encrypted when being transmitted. You will see that you are entering a secure area when the URL to the left of the colon changes from http to https. Also, your browser may have a special symbol, like a key or a lock. Having a secure server takes the risk out of your credit card transactions. If at any time you do not feel comfortable providing your credit card information while shopping online, please contact your Signet® customer care team via phone at 800-654-3889.

Other Information

When you visit this site, we automatically recognize some information about you, including your Internet service provider. We do NOT automatically recognize your name, address, e-mail address or any other information specific to you. This site only tracks how many times the site is visited and what features are used to ensure the site is meeting needs.
