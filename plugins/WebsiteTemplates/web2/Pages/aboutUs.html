About Us

For over 40 years, Signet, Inc. remains a comprehensive outsource provider of logo merchandise, apparel and related services partnering with regional and national companies to enhance client revenues, culture building and cost savings activities.

Signet, Inc. is a WBENC certified woman-owned business, and ranks in the top 100 companies in our industry.

Signet, Inc. is the contracted supplier for all ServiceMaster Business Units' promotional products and apparel. We maintain a reputation for high quality products and services, timely delivery, and creative assistance. We have a complete customer care team dedicated to ServiceMaster who are ready and waiting to assist you.

To learn more about us, please visit www.gosignet.com .
