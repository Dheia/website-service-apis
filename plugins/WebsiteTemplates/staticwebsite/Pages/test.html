TLINK GPS Watch





Get precise front, middle, and back of the green yardages using Bluetooth Low Energy Technology to communicate with your smartphone. Simply open up the GPS app on your smartphone, connect the TLINK to your phone and that's it!.
TLINK is the lightest GPS watch on the market, weighing only 30 grams!, allowing you to play without even noticing it.

 

 


TLINK fits into both a wristband and a belt clip, allowing your yardages to be only a quick glance away.
TLINK lasts 5 rounds on a single charge and only uses about 20% of yoru smartphone battery life per round.
